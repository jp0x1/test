Why GitBad?

Welcome to GitBad, the only platform where writing bad code is not just allowed—it's encouraged!

Because copy-pasting from ChatGPT is an art form. Because our linter is just a random number generator. Because "works on my machine" is our only QA process. Because we believe in Error-driven development.

Join GitBad and help us redefine what "bad code" really means.
May your flags be plentiful and your code reviews merciless!
